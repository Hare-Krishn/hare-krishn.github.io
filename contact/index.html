Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We provide complete exhibition solutions including stall design, 3D visualization, construction, setup, and dismantling services.

How long does it take to design and build a stall?

Typically 2-4 weeks depending on complexity. Rush projects can be accommodated with advance notice.

Do you work outside Mumbai?

Yes! We serve clients across India and internationally. We handle logistics and local coordination.

Absolutely! We provide detailed 3D renderings and virtual walkthroughs before construction begins.

Our packages include design, materials, construction, setup, graphics, lighting, and dismantling services.

Yes, we provide on-site support and maintenance throughout your exhibition period.
